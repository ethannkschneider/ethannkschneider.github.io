About Me

I'm an NYC-based web developer specializing in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React, and Redux. I love building things and writing pretty code.

I have a B.A. in Math and Philosophy from Yale University ('14) and have worked in the education and non-profit sectors. When I'm not coding, I love drumming, being outdoors, and speaking Mandarin.
